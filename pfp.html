About The Professor

Dr. Alex White

Dr. White is a VT professor in the Dairy Science department, but he has been teaching for 25 years at multiple schools. He did his PhD research here at Virginia Tech on retirement planning strategies, specifically for agricultural producers and small business owners. He spends his time outside of teaching running a BBQ business with his brother, and driving his Jeep, which he never forgets to mention in class.

About The Course

In Personal Financial Planning, we learn how to plan for our financial futures. We focus on the basics of measuring financial health, the time value of money, taxes, credit, investing, insurance, and retirement planning. Our grades are based on homework, three exams, and a final.
